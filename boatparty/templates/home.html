The Proposal

After a short train ride up Uetliberg Mountain and an even shorter hike, Nick and Natalie found themselves sitting on a bench, taking in a beautiful view of Zurich. Nick pretended to set up a camera on a tripod for a self-timed photo but really, he began recording a video. While he had planned a speech similar to a recoupling speech on Love Island, nerves got him and all he could get out was “Will you be my companion forever?” After some pictures, the two made their way to celebrate at the most on brand place possible, a Beer garden.
